Public forks and modified builds must use a clearly different name and must replace the proprietary Games branding assets unless written permission is granted.

Examples of acceptable fork naming:

- `My Board Games Fork`
- `Open Classic Games`
- `Games Community Edition by <name>`

Examples of restricted naming:

- `Games`
- `Official Games`
- `Games Pro`
- names that mainly add a small suffix while keeping confusing official branding
